Scenario II:

After a morning’s hard work, you decide to keep playing the game. However, you have an appointment with your best friend this afternoon and you don't want to miss it.

This time, you will continue using your smartwatch to check time and money raising progress.

Press shift key to see the interface. You need to check the time on your smartwatch to make sure that you won’t miss the appointment.

Your tasks:

  • Play the game
  • Check the time
  • Check the raising money progress
  • Answer questions about the above tasks. The questions will automatically pop up and stop the game.
